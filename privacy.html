Privacy Policy — Last updated March 5, 2026

Arete ("the App") is a personal excellence app. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

Information We Collect

The App collects the following information:

  • Your first name — entered during setup, stored locally on your device only.
  • Personal profile information — background, goals, and reflections; stored locally.
  • Journal entries — stored locally on your device only.
  • Messages to the Cabinet — messages and relevant profile context are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate responses.

How We Use Your Information

  • Your profile and journal data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • When you use the Cabinet feature, your message and a summary of profile context are sent to Anthropic; see Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data for advertising.

Data Storage

Personal data is stored locally (AsyncStorage). Deleting the app removes local data.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
