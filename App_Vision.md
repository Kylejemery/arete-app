# Arete — App Vision

## Tagline
*"Be who you want to be."*

## Mission
Arete is an AI-powered mobile application that helps people pursue excellence — in their work, their health, their relationships, and their inner life. It is built for an age when AI is transforming the economy and many people are left searching for meaning, direction, and identity without the structure that traditional work once provided. Arete gives them a framework, a coach, and a community of invisible counselors who know them deeply and hold them to their highest standard.

The app is rooted in Stoic philosophy — not as an aesthetic, but as a living practice. It asks users every day: *Are you becoming who you want to be?*

---

## Core Concept — The Cabinet of Invisible Counselors
Inspired by Napoleon Hill's concept of the Cabinet of Invisible Counselors, Arete allows each user to assemble a personal cabinet of admired figures — historical, fictional, or imagined future selves — who serve as AI-powered coaches and accountability partners. The cabinet knows the user deeply, speaks in the voices of the chosen figures, and engages with the real texture of the user's daily life.

---

## Target User
- People who are serious about personal growth and self-improvement
- People searching for meaning and direction — especially in a world disrupted by AI
- People who want more than a habit tracker — they want a coach, a mirror, and a philosophy
- People drawn to Stoicism, mindfulness, and intentional living
- Athletes, builders, thinkers, parents — people who are trying to be excellent at multiple things simultaneously

## Feature Roadmap

### Phase 1 — MVP
- User onboarding (name, goals, cabinet selection)
- Morning check-in (daily intention → cabinet responds)
- Evening debrief (reflection → cabinet responds)
- Cabinet chat (open conversation anytime)
- Push notifications (morning & evening reminders)
- Basic user profile (goals, cabinet members)

### Phase 2 — Tracking
- Habit & goal tracking (daily inputs, streaks)
- Exercise & activity tracker (log workouts)
- Nutrition tracker (meals, hydration)
- Work block tracker (Pomodoro-style focus sessions)
- Progress dashboard (visualize trends)

### Phase 3 — Growth
- Reading tracker (log books, reading timer)
- Reading assignments (cabinet suggests books)
- Advanced analytics (weekly/monthly reviews)
- Accountability partner (share progress with a friend)
- Cabinet customization (users build their own cabinet)

## The User Experience — A Day in Arete

**7:00 AM** — Push notification: *"Marcus Aurelius is waiting. How will you use this day?"*
User opens app → Morning check-in screen → Types their intentions for the day → Cabinet responds with coaching, questions, and encouragement.

**Throughout the day** — User logs work blocks, meals, exercise. Optional mid-day cabinet check-in.

**9:00 PM** — Push notification: *"The day is ending. Did you live it well?"*
User opens app → Evening debrief screen → Reflects on the day → Cabinet responds honestly — celebrating wins, naming shortfalls, extracting lessons.

**Anytime** — User can open the cabinet chat and have a full conversation with their counselors about any topic — a decision, a struggle, a win, a question.

## Design Principles
1. **No sycophancy** — The AI should challenge, not flatter
2. **Stoic foundation** — Every interaction grounded in Stoic principles
3. **Personal and deep** — The app should feel like it truly knows you
4. **Simple and focused** — Beautiful, minimal UI. No clutter
5. **Honest and firm** — The cabinet tells the truth, always
6. **Built for the long game** — Daily use, compounding over time
